About Us

👋 About Boni Bytes EduTech

Welcome to Boni Bytes EduTech — Where Learning Meets Innovation! 🚀

📍 Based in Tiruvannamalai, Tamil Nadu, Boni Bytes EduTech is a forward-thinking educational technology startup founded in 2023 by Rahulprasad Veeramani. We are on a mission to transform the way students and professionals gain knowledge, skills, and real-world experience.

🎯 Our Mission

To empower learners through practical, hands-on education that prepares them for the challenges of the modern world.

💡 What We Offer

  • 🛠️ Live Projects – Tackle real-world problems to build confidence and experience.
  • 🧠 Skill-Based Workshops – Learn by doing with interactive, practical sessions.
  • 🧑‍🏫 Expert-Led Webinars – Stay updated with industry trends from professionals.
  • 📈 Career-Focused Guidance – From beginners to job seekers, we help shape your future.

👥 Who We Are

We’re a passionate team of educators, developers, and innovators working together to create meaningful learning experiences. With a growing network of learners and professionals, we strive to make tech education accessible, engaging, and effective.
